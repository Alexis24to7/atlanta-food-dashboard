Welcome to the FWA Dashboard

Food Well Alliance (FWA) is a collaborative network that supports over 300 community gardens, urban farms, and orchards across metro Atlanta.

This project was developed to help Atlanta citizens find resources to build thriving, equitable communities through local food. Our mission is to connect you with the tools, land, and people needed to grow food where you live.

How to Rent Tools

FWA partners with the Atlanta ToolBank. To access the Urban Agriculture Tool Lending Program, you must first complete a BCS Tractor Training session.

Requesting Compost

Registered community gardens can request compost deliveries and mechanical assistance directly through the FWA Resource Center.
